## Presentation slides

### Foundation

As explained in the Pillars section of the site (please go through them if you haven't), each of our 4 pillars has its own visual theme. We have blank templates corresponding to each of the 4 pillar themes. We also have a base deck that has been populated with content for introducing the Interledger Foundation.

This collection of templates is recommended for presentations targeted at audiences who are interested in the Foundation and the work that we do.
